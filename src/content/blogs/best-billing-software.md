## Key Features of Modern Billing Software

A good billing solution should offer more than just invoice generation. It should help you manage your entire business from one dashboard.

### 1. Fast Invoice Generation

Modern POS billing systems allow you to create invoices in seconds.  
For example, Kravy allows businesses to generate bills in **just 3 clicks**, which significantly reduces checkout time.

### 2. GST Ready Billing

For Indian businesses, GST compliance is important. A smart billing system automatically calculates taxes and generates GST-ready invoices, saving time and avoiding calculation mistakes.

### 3. Inventory Management

Billing software can automatically track stock when items are sold. This means you always know:

- Which products are selling fast  
- Which items are low in stock  
- When to reorder inventory

### 4. Real-Time Sales Tracking

With a smart dashboard, business owners can view daily sales, payment methods, and performance insights in real time. This helps you understand how your business is performing every day.

### 5. Multiple Payment Options

Today customers pay using different methods such as:

- Cash  
- UPI  
- Card payments  

A modern POS system allows businesses to accept multiple payment methods and track them easily.
